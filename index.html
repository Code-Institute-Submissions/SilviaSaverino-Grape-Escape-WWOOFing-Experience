What is WWOOFing?

WWOOFing stands for "World Wide Opportunities on Organic Farms".
The volunteer or wwoofer gets the opportunity to spend time in an organic farm, learn what it's like, how it works and what sustainable agriculture is all about.

During your stay in the farm
- alongside wwoofers from other Countries and with visits ranging from a few days to several months -
the host will provide accommodation and opportunities to learn about organic and self-sufficient lifestyles;
in returns of an average of four to six hours farming or gardening activities.

Please, note this is not a monetary exchange program, but merely an educational one.
You might find some helpful information about WWOOFing in our #FAQs# page!

About Us

Torsi Villa

The Torsi family villa was built in the 1870, originally on a land of just 50 yards with just couple of olives trees and a small barn at the back. Today this stunning villa comprises all the commodities of the 21st century as well as some original features too.

Accommodation will be provided across its 12rooms to all of our lovely guests grape-escaping from the city buzz and a frenetic lifestyle, or willing to join us to simply enjoy some good wine and time in the Italian countryside.

What we offer

No special skills are needed to start this little journey of yours and possibly to form long lasting friendships with the other guests along the way.

Between grape harvesting sessions you will be able to enjoy morning coffee, Italian pastries, charcuteries, wine tasting and wine pairing with some of the best organic product and regional delicatessen,
plus weekends excursions in the nearby cities, villages and more.

Get involved

Either as a beginner or a Wwoofing veteran all that the Torsi family wishes for you is to have a great time and get involved in their small community to really capture the essence of this beautiful Italian region, whilst learning about grape harvesting and wine

Although we won't hide that nowadays the Villa needs some extra love and attention, and we will gratefully welcome little renovation works from any volunteers that wish to do so.


We pride ourselves for the sense of belongings we provide to each and one of our wwoofers and are happy that many had stayed in touch with us through all these years!
